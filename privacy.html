Privacy Logic & Data Protocols

01. Data Collection Strategy

We collect minimal personal data—specifically your email address and authentication credentials—solely to provide access to your workspace and maintain your credit balance. We do not sell or lease your identity to third-party entities.

02. Neural Input Privacy

The messages you send to the chat module and the prompts used in the creation module are processed through the Google Gemini API. This data is used to generate responses. We do not store your specific prompts for training external visual or linguistic models.

03. Cookies & Advertising

We use standard browser cookies to maintain your login session. Additionally, we use Google AdSense to serve advertisements. Google may use DART cookies to serve ads based on your visit to this and other websites on the internet. You may opt-out of these cookies by visiting the Google Ad and Content Network privacy policy.

04. Encryption Standards

All communication between your terminal and our synthesis cores is protected via industry-standard SSL encryption, ensuring your creative thoughts remain confidential.
